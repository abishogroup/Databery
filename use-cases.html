SDRs & BDRs

Prospecting & outbound
Best with Account Intelligence
The challenge

Reps either burn selling hours researching accounts one by one, or skip it and blast a generic list. Either way, reply rates crater — there's no reason for the prospect to care, and no way to tell which accounts are worth the effort.

How Databery helps
  • Every account arrives pre-researched, with an intent score so reps work the hottest first.
  • A ready-to-send outreach hook per account — why now, and the line that earns a reply.
  • The right people in the buying group, so reps reach out to someone who'll care.

Account Executives

Closing complex deals
Best with Stakeholder Intel
The challenge

Big deals get single-threaded around one friendly contact. Then a hidden decision-maker appears, or a competitor turns out to be already inside — and the forecast slips a quarter for reasons nobody saw coming.

How Databery helps
  • A full map of the buying group — who's economic buyer, champion, and blocker.
  • A profile and tailored angle for each stakeholder you need to win over.
  • A read on which competitor is in the deal and how to counter them.

RevOps

Pipeline & process
Best with Signal Monitoring
The challenge

Account data is patchy and inconsistent, "intent" feeds are noisy, and reps prioritise by gut. Routing, scoring, and forecasting all suffer because the inputs feeding the funnel aren't trustworthy.

How Databery helps
  • Consistent, scored intelligence on every account — clean inputs for your funnel.
  • A live trigger feed that flows into your CRM and powers routing and prioritisation.
  • Evidence behind every "hot" flag, so leadership trusts the forecast.

Demand & account marketing
Best with Account Intelligence

How Databery helps
  • A sharp, researched target list both teams agree on and work together.

Sales Leadership

Forecasting & competitive edge
Best with Win-Loss Intelligence
The challenge

Forecasts rest on gut feel, the team keeps losing to the same rival, and there's no systematic way to learn from closed deals. The same mistakes repeat because nobody captures why deals really go the way they do.

How Databery helps
  • Win-loss analysis that surfaces the real decision criteria behind wins and losses.
  • Live competitive intelligence and battlecards that lift win-rate against key rivals.
