Who we are

We do the research your reps never have time for.

Databery is a senior-led account & sales intelligence firm. We exist for one reason: to put a researched, scored, ready-to-act brief in front of every seller — so no good account is ever worked blind.

What we believe

Selling shouldn't start with a blank page. Every account a rep touches already has a story — a new leader, a fresh round, a quiet migration. Our job is to find that story and hand it over before the call, so your team spends its hours closing, not digging.

Why we exist

Founded on a simple frustration

After years inside B2B sales and research teams, our founder kept seeing the same waste: talented reps burning hours hunting for context, or skipping it entirely and pitching blind. Data tools promised a fix, but only handed over more fields to sift.

Databery was built to close that gap — not another dashboard, but real research, done by senior analysts, scored for intent, and written in language a seller can act on the same day.

Today we partner with revenue teams across SaaS, fintech, and enterprise to turn their target lists into pipeline, one deeply researched account at a time.
