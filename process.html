How it works

From target list to sales-ready intelligence, in five stages.

No black boxes. You'll always know which accounts we're working, what we've found, and what your reps should do next.

01
Day 1 · Alignment

Define the target

We start with your ICP and the accounts that matter most. Together we agree what a "good" account looks like, which signals count, and what your reps need to walk into a call.

ICP reviewAccount listSignal priorities
02
Day 1-3 · Research

Research each account

Analysts go deep on every target, covering financials, hiring, tech stack, leadership, news, and public buyer activity, pulling only what's relevant to your deal.

FirmographicsSignal huntingStakeholder mapping
03
Day 3-4 · Scoring

Score & prioritise

We turn raw findings into an intent score and a clear priority order, so your team spends its hours on the accounts most likely to move now.

Intent scoringPrioritisationTiming reads
04
Day 4-5 · Packaging

Build the outreach hooks

For each account we write the angle: why now, who to contact, what they care about, and the opening that earns a reply, ready to paste into your sequence.

Outreach anglesMessaging hooksTalk tracks
05
Day 5 · Handover

Deliver & (optionally) monitor

You receive a clean, sales-ready intelligence pack and a live walkthrough. From there we can keep monitoring the list for new triggers on a retainer.

Intelligence packWalkthroughOngoing monitoring
