General

Process & delivery

How it runs

Most engagements kick off within a few days of our first call. For a hot account or a time-sensitive deal, we offer an expedited track that can begin within 48 hours.

Account briefs typically land in 3-5 days. Larger projects like GTM Research run over a couple of weeks. You'll always get a committed delivery date up front, and we hold ourselves to it.

Sales-ready formats your team will actually use, a clean brief, a CRM-ready sheet, a stakeholder map, or a living battlecard. No 80-page PDFs nobody reopens. We'll match the format to your workflow.
